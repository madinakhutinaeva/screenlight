# Privacy Policy

**App name:** Screenlight  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-24

This Privacy Policy explains how **Screenlight** (the “App”) handles information.

## 1. Developer / Contact
**Developer:** Madina Khutinaeva 
**Contact email:** madinakhutinaeva@gmail.com

## 2. Information the App collects
The App **does not collect, store, or share** personal information.

The App does not require registration, does not provide account features, and does not send user data to the developer’s servers.

## 3. Device permissions
The App may request the following permission(s) to function:

- **Camera / Flash (CAMERA)** — used **only** to turn the device flashlight on/off (control the flash).  
  The App **does not** take photos or record video, and it does not access camera content for any purpose other than controlling the flashlight.

> Note: The exact list of permissions may vary depending on Android version and the App implementation.

## 4. Sharing of information
The App **does not share** user information with third parties.

## 5. Ads and analytics
The App **does not include** advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs (for example, AdMob/Firebase) and does not collect identifiers or events for analytics purposes.

If ads or analytics are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

## 6. Data security
Because the App does not collect or store user data, there is no user data storage on the developer’s side.

## 7. Children’s privacy
The App is not specifically directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at the same GitHub Pages link.
